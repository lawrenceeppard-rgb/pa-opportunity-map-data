Our Purpose

These are difficult times in the United States. Trust in people and institutions is declining. Our democracy is under serious strain.

High-quality, consistently nonpartisan information — in service of the American public.

In light of this, we have created this Institute as a way to disseminate high-quality and consistently nonpartisan information to the American public around issues of societal well-being, democracy promotion, and news literacy.

In memoriam

Thomas Connors

The Connors Institute is named for the late Thomas Connors, a longtime editor for the Journal of Commerce newspaper and a dedicated advocate for disability rights in the 1960s and 1970s in Fairfax, Virginia.

His commitment to honest, rigorous journalism and his tireless civic service continue to inspire the work of this Institute.
